About me!

My love for programming began when I was introduced to C, C++ and HTML in the 10th grade. Having only surfed the webpages on internet, it amazed me how writing some syntax in a logical and organized way can result into a webpage or an application. Since then, I wanted to become a software developer and thus acquired the 4 year Engineering degree.

I love logical thinking and coming up with better ways of solving the same problem and thus solving bugs in my code gives me more reasons to think all the possibilities and search and learn new ways of solving problems. When am not on my laptop coding, I like to paint, sketch, go hiking or just watch some TV shows. I like playing word games and learning new board games. I love traveling and want to explore more places.
