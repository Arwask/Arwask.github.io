Why I chose to be a developer?

My love for programming began when I was introduced to C, C++ and HTML in the 10th grade. Having only surfed the webpages on internet, it amazed me how writing some syntax in a logical and organized way can result into a webpage or an application. Since then, I just knew that I wanted to become a software developer and thus acquired the 4 year Engineering degree.

I love logical thinking and coming up with efficient ways of solving the same problem. Solving bugs in my code gives me more reasons to think of all the possibilities as well as search and learn new things and new ways of solving problems. When am not on my laptop coding, I like to paint, sketch, go hiking or just watch some TV shows. I like playing word games and learning new board games. I love traveling and want to explore more places.

An app made entirely in Reactjs with help of React-dom. It lets users socialize with their friends. A user can login to see any public and private posts and events from their friends. They can create new public/private posts and new events, search for friends and posts by them using keywords.
